Pesto

Description

Pesto is a green italian sauce, its rich, nutty taste makes it a great option to add to pastas, salads or sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 3 cups packed fresh basil leaves
  • Â¼ cup pine nuts, almost any kind of nut is fine
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice

Steps

  1. Thinly chop all ingredients and squeeze a half a lemon;
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a blender cup;
  3. Blend until it forms a light green paste;
  4. Enjoy!You may store it in the fridge for a few days or eat it imeadiatly.
